Privacy Policy

MeQR is basically a local little tool for keeping QR codes tidy. The QR code contents, profile names, subtitles, avatars, backgrounds, and style settings you add are stored on your device by default, and they are not uploaded to my server or used for ads, analytics, tracking, or any other suspicious side quest. If you pick images from Photos, import a QR image, or save a share card to Photos, the app only uses the content you chose for that exact thing. If you export, screenshot, or share something yourself, then yes, that content has left the app's tiny room and will be handled by whatever system feature or app you sent it to. There is no account system, no in-app ads, no third-party analytics SDK, and no user tracking right now. iCloud sync / backup is also not officially live yet, so please do not assume your data is automatically going to the cloud; if that changes, I will update this page.
