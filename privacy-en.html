Privacy Policy

MeQR helps you create, manage, display, and exchange social QR-code profiles. Data you create in the app, such as profile names, descriptions, avatars, background images, QR-code contents, platform information, appearance settings, and encounter records, is generally stored locally on your device and used to provide the app's editing, display, import, scanning, and photo-saving features.

When you use the online MeQR exchange-code feature, the app uploads the information needed to make that exchange code work. This may include the profile name, description, avatar image, selected platform names, and the corresponding QR-code contents you choose to share. Other MeQR users can then scan your code and retrieve the shared profile from the server. The code may also contain a small offline fallback profile so basic information can still be shown when the network is unavailable. Please do not include information in an exchange code that you do not want other people to see or save.

Camera and Photos access are used only for actions you start, such as scanning a code, importing a QR image, choosing an avatar or background image, or saving an exchange-code image to Photos. The app does not read your photo library in the background and does not upload photos you did not select. MeQR currently does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app/cross-site tracking.

Profile data uploaded for online exchange codes is used to provide the exchange-code service and may remain on the server for a period of time so the code continues to work. You can update future sharing by editing local data and generating a new exchange code. Content that another person has already scanned, saved, screenshotted, or forwarded is no longer fully controlled by the app.

If account features, sync, backup, paid services, or new data-processing features are added later, this policy will be updated accordingly. By using those features, you acknowledge that the necessary data will be handled as described in this policy.
